Instagram — What is collected

  • Account info: username, profile photo, phone
  • User content: posts, stories, DMs
  • Contacts you upload
  • Device identifiers, IP, cookies, pixels
  • Location (GPS if allowed), usage & analytics
  • Camera & photo access when granted
  • Data from Meta affiliates, advertisers, third-party apps

If something goes wrong

  1. Change password & enable 2FA
  2. Remove connected apps & check account activity
  3. Report impersonation and document screenshots
Risk Level Key
High — Major collection & sharing
Collects identity, device & analytics; shares across Meta services & advertisers; used for ads, profiling and cross-app tracking.
Medium — Moderate collection
Collects many data types but sharing stays mostly in Meta ecosystem and with partners.
Low — Minimal collection
Only essential data; limited external sharing.
