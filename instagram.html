Platform Comparison

Quick view of how platforms compare on collection, sharing, and transparency.

Risk Level Key
What the colors mean
High — Major collection & sharing
Collects identity, messages, location, biometrics, browsing & device data and shares broadly with advertisers, affiliates, or partners. Used for ads, profiling, and AI training — higher exposure if breached.
Medium — Moderate collection
Collects many data types (device, usage, contacts) but shares less externally or limits retention/cross-platform tracking.
Low — Minimal collection
Only essential account data; limited tracking and sharing.
