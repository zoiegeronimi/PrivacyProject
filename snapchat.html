Snapchat — What is collected

  • Account info (username, phone, email)
  • Snaps, Stories, messages, Memories
  • Contacts you upload
  • Linked apps (Bitmoji, Snap Kit), ad/analytics partners
  • Device details, IP, cookies, sensors
  • Approximate/precise location (GPS/Wi-Fi)
  • Engagement patterns, lens/filter usage
Risk Level Key
High — Major collection & sharing
Collects identity & device data and shares with advertisers & analytics partners; used for ads and measurement.
Medium — Moderate collection
Collects location & engagement data; shares with partners but often retains data for shorter periods and has fewer cross-app integrations.
Low — Minimal collection
Only essential data and limited sharing.
