TikTok — What is collected

(Policy applies to users 13+)

  • Account info (name, age, email)
  • User content: videos, comments, messages
  • Payment details, survey responses, ID verification docs
  • Linked accounts (Google, Facebook), advertisers, affiliates, public records
  • Device details, IP, cookies, metadata, engagement patterns
  • Approximate location (U.S.), clipboard (with permission)
  • Biometric identifiers (face/voice) with consent

If your account is compromised

  1. Change password & enable 2FA immediately
  2. Revoke third-party app access and unlink accounts
  3. Report impersonation to TikTok support & document evidence
Risk Level Key
High — Major collection & sharing
Collects identity, messages, contacts, biometrics, device & browsing data; shares with advertisers, affiliates, and partners; used for ad personalization & AI training.
Medium — Moderate collection
Collects many data types but may limit external sharing relative to highest-risk platforms.
Low — Minimal collection
Only essential data and limited sharing.
