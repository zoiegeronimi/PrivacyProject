Risk Key
High — Major data collection & sharing: identity, device & browsing data, messages/contacts/biometrics, used for personalization/AI and shared with advertisers/partners.
Medium — Moderate collection & sharing: many personal data types but fewer third-party integrations and more user controls/shorter retention.
Low — Minimal collection: essential account data only; limited sharing and stronger user control.
